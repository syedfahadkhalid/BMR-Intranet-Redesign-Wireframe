BioMed Realty announces new iPhone/iPad app. Great tool for every professional in life science real estate industry

The Biomed Realty app delivers an interactive and comprehensive view of all Biomed Realty's properties in the core life science markets and Wexford Science & technology's diverse university platform. the app provides vital information about all of these state-of-the-art laboratory and office buildings, while getting a full understanding of the benefits and amenities available at these locations. the app allows you to easily find and share the information you want about these properties. this is a must have app for any professional in the life science real estate industry.
